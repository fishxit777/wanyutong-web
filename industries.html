// 適用產業// Use Cases

最適合跨語言現場團隊Built for Multilingual Work Sites

萬語通優先服務已經用 LINE 管人的台灣雇主，尤其是工廠、工地、看護、餐飲、農場與排班現場。WanyuTong is designed for Taiwan employers already managing teams through LINE, especially factories, construction sites, care work, restaurants, farms, and shift teams.

判斷是否適合Best Fit

如果你的團隊每天在 LINE 交辦、確認、請假、付款、排班或公告，萬語通會比一般翻譯 App 更貼近流程。If your team uses LINE for tasks, confirmations, leave, payments, schedules, or notices, WanyuTong fits better than a standalone translation app.

工廠與製造業Factories

工安通知、機台異常、品質規範、加班與交期確認都需要看得懂又留得住。Safety notices, machine issues, quality rules, overtime, and deadlines need clarity and records.

工地與工程團隊Construction

集合時間、安全提醒、材料規格、臨時變更，都適合用雙語群組流程處理。Meeting times, safety reminders, material specs, and urgent changes need bilingual group workflows.

看護與家庭照護Caregiving

用藥、回診、作息與臨時狀況要講清楚，降低家屬與看護之間的誤會。Medication, appointments, routines, and urgent care updates must be clear for families and caregivers.

餐飲與排班現場Restaurants and Shifts

排班、衛生、備料、收貨與日常交辦重複發生，適合固定流程。Scheduling, hygiene, prep, receiving, and daily tasks repeat often and benefit from a fixed process.

農場與臨時人力Farms and Temporary Teams

採收、集合、天候、工具與工作區域通知，能用簡單指令快速同步。Harvest, meeting points, weather, tools, and work zones can be synchronized with simple commands.

多群組管理Multiple Groups

同時管理多個班別或案場時，摘要、報表與紀錄查詢會比單純翻譯更重要。When managing several teams, summaries, reports, and searchable records matter more than translation alone.
