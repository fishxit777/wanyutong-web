功能不是越多越好，是要真的用得懂Features That Stay Usable

萬語通把翻譯、語音、圖片 OCR、AI 摘要、每日報表與紀錄查詢整理成同一套 LINE 工作流程，讓現場不用學新 App。WanyuTong combines translation, voice, image OCR, AI summaries, daily reports, and searchable records inside the LINE workflow your team already uses.

最重要的差異Core Difference

不是單次翻譯工具，而是讓主管、外籍員工與客服協助都能沿著同一條流程確認事情。It is not a one-off translator. It keeps supervisors, foreign workers, and support follow-up inside one traceable workflow.

LINE 群組雙向翻譯Group Translation

主管用中文交代，員工看到自己的語言。混合國籍群組也能維持同一個工作節奏。Supervisors write in Chinese while workers read in their language, keeping mixed-language groups aligned.

語音與圖片 OCRVoice and OCR

語音訊息、公告照片、班表、合約截圖都能轉成可理解文字，減少重新打字。Voice messages, notices, schedules, and screenshots become readable text without manual retyping.

AI 摘要與每日報表AI Summary and Reports

把群組重點、待辦、付款、請假、異常整理出來，交接時不用整段往回翻。Summaries extract tasks, payments, leave requests, and issues so managers do not scroll through everything.

紀錄查詢與匯出Search and Export

關鍵字可查詢，統計可匯出，適合交接、補款、對帳與主管回報。Searchable records and exports support handover, reconciliation, and management reporting.

人工複核提醒Human Review Reminders

遇到工安、金額、合約、醫療等高風險內容時，提醒使用者自行確認。High-risk safety, payment, contract, or medical content is flagged for human review.

免費先用，滿意再升級Start Free

免費版可先跑基本文字翻譯；需要完整語言、語音、OCR、摘要與報表再升級。Start with basic text translation, then upgrade for full language coverage, voice, OCR, summaries, and reports.
